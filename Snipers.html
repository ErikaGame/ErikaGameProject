Snipers

Kevin

A special soldier of the unit: "USA-Snipers S" Soladt named Kevin was an ordinary soldier he loved his country but during a military operation he was discovered by an enemy sniper and gave orders to the troops to catch the sniper Kevin. After that he was brought with grenades around his body and the explosion was visible even from the satellites of America. After that he was taken away by a secret group to develop super soldiers and at the moment Kevin is the best sniper in America.
